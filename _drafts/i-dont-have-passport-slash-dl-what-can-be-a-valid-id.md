# I don't have Passport/DL. What can be a valid id?

You can bring any one from the listed identity cards (original only)  
- Voter Identity Card  
- Passport  
- PAN Card  
- Driving License  
- Photo ID card issued by Central / State Govt  
- Nationalised Bank Passbook with photograph
